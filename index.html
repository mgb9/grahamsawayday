  • Tickets bought at face value through official club sites
  • 16 May weekend — out
  • Milan city break — been there, done that (San Siro only as a backup game)
  • Graham-checked against the Wolves fixture list (Championship 2026-27)
  • Melissa's terms: cheap, and a considerate weekend — she's home with the children

  • Bad dates: 5 & 26 January — the weekends touching them (2–4 & 23–25 Jan) are out
  • Sunday 14 February — Valentine's Day, with half term starting Monday. See the Milan derby card for how badly the calendar wants to test you
How to read the scores — the key
AtmosphereHow big the football occasion is — noise, stakes, stadium. 5 = hairs-on-neck European night stuff · 1 = quiet afternoon.
TicketsOdds of face-value tickets from the official club shop. 5 = buy online, no fuss, no membership · 1 = members-only instant sellout. The 🎟 line on each card says exactly where to buy, when the sale opens and rough prices.
MelissaSign-off odds. She's staying home with the children, so this scores total cost plus considerate timing. 5 = cheap trip on a quiet weekend · 1 = expensive and/or dreadful timing — abroad on Mothering Sunday (7 March) or the last weekend of the school holidays.
ValueWhole-weekend cost: flights + match tickets + hotels + food. 5 = cheap as chips · 1 = premium-priced weekend.
GrahamThe Wolves clash check, against the 2026-27 Championship fixture list. 5 = Wolves away or season over — Molineux is dark, Graham travels guilt-free · 3 = missable home game (Lincoln, with respect) · 1 = Birmingham City at Molineux. He's not coming, don't ask. The 🐺 line names the fixture.
Tot /25All five added together — the table view's default order. Maximum 25; anything over 21 is a serious contender.

Small print — worth reading

Kick-off days aren't set yet. Leagues fix the weekend now but confirm Saturday-or-Sunday only later: Italy & Germany ~4–6 weeks ahead, Spain ~2 weeks, Portugal barely 10 days. For two-game weekends, book the trip around the weekend and pick your pair once days are announced. Weekends with 3–4 candidate games are the safest bets.

Tickets: buy only from official club shops (linked in our chat notes). Most open general sale 1–4 weeks before kick-off, no membership needed, except the marked hard tickets. Avoid viagogo-type resellers — clubs void those.

Scores: Atmosphere = the football occasion · Tickets = odds of face-value seats (the 🎟 line on each card says exactly where, when and for how much) · Melissa = sign-off odds — cost plus considerate timing while she's home with the kids · Value = flights + tickets + hotels combined · Graham = Wolves clash check — 5 wolves means Molineux is dark that weekend, 1 means it's derby day and he's not coming.

The nearly-missed list: the EU's biometric Entry/Exit System is live — allow extra time at passport control, especially for early flights. ETIAS (the €20 EU travel authorisation for UK citizens) launches around late 2026 and may be mandatory by April 2027 — apply only at the official EU site, travel-europe.europa.eu/etias; reminder set for 1 Feb 2027. Passports must be issued within 10 years and valid 3+ months beyond the return date — check now. Como, Benfica and Porto may be in European or domestic cup runs that can move league fixtures (17–18 Apr sits safely between the Champions League QFs and semis; late-April/May weekends carry more risk). The 06:15 Porto→Stansted flight needs a pre-booked ~€25 taxi (metro starts too late) and a 2-hour airport arrival. Italian match tickets are named per person — have every traveller's passport details ready and buy all seats in one transaction. And double-check Melissa's own school's 2026-27 calendar — academies sometimes differ from the county dates.

The Tuesday option (pending Melissa): if a Sat–Tue window is approved, Monday-night fixtures become watchable rather than a scheduling risk, and returns get cheaper and kinder — flagged here so no option is scored on it until it's approved.

January and February flights are already on sale — winter-window trips can be booked at today's prices, unlike the spring options that wait on Ryanair's summer release. Winter caveats: kick-off days for Jan–Feb rounds get confirmed from ~Nov–Dec 2026, Wolves' FA Cup third-round fate (weekend of 9–10 Jan) is known at the December draw, and northern Europe in January is 0–6°C and damp — pack accordingly.

Flight times are current-season schedules — the routes are real but exact timings for spring 2027 firm up when each carrier publishes its season; the November flight reminder re-checks them.
